# Limitations and Next Steps

## Limitations
The model is linear, static within each run, and built on fixed technical coefficients. It does not include endogenous price adjustment, capacity constraints, or strategic firm behavior. Damping is a proxy, not a directly observed mechanism.

## Measurement caveats
The institutional proxy relies on logistics performance scores, and the structural proxy relies on concentration in the input network. These are defensible choices, but they remain proxies.

## Next steps
The thesis can be strengthened further by adding:
- confidence intervals around major outputs
- additional shock scenarios
- alternative damping specifications
- comparison with historical disruptions where available

## Presentation framing
In a defense, the right claim is not that the model is complete. The right claim is that it is conceptually coherent, empirically grounded, and robust enough to support the thesis argument.
